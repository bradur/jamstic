The Crystal Tower

Zarguuf is on a holiday somewhere south, staying in a very nice tower. For some reason the local goblins desperately want to stay in the same tower! Stop the goblins by powering up the nearby magic turrets with some crystal energy sources.

Important!

We recommend the Windows build - the WebGL build has some issues with mouse panning especially if you have multiple monitors.

How to play

  • Look at the minimap to see where goblins are coming from.
  • Select an energy source by pressing number keys 1-4 or by clicking.
  • Connect the energy source to a turret. The connection is not instant, the energy source will travel to the turret.
  • The turret will shoot at goblins within its range if it has an energy source connected.
  • You can change the connection to another turret later on! Simply select the energy source again and choose some other turret.
  • You can connect at most 2 energy sources per turret.
  • There are 4 different energy sources and they each produce an unique effect. Ice for example slows down enemies.
  • Combinations of the energy sources also provide unique combinations.
  • There is a wait time between each goblin wave. This can be skipped to gain a score multiplier for a time.
  • Hover over the tower icons to see what the currently connected energy sources do.
  • You may click & drag on the minimap to change the view.

Keys
