Smoke Signals Everyday

Smoke Signals Everyday

In Smoke Signals Everyday, you play a raccoon who is in charge of sending smoke signals for his raccoon village. You follow certain waves to produce different signals and try to get understood. Keys: Up and Down arrows - Move the paw Space - Hold down to use nitro while moving the paw Esc - Pause game / open menu Q - Quit game while in pause menu
