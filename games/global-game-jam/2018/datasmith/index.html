Datasmith

Datasmith

Tower defense with a computer twist. In this 3D tower defense game you're playing as a little Chip whose responsibility is to transmit data inside a computer. First step is to transfer a processor by constructing a chain of transmitter nodes. After powering the processor, you have to build nodes to the data target and protect them from attacking viruses. You can build extra nodes for the viruses to distract them or whack at them with your trusty hammer!
