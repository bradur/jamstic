Simian Situation

Simian Situation is a game where you play as a monkey defending its village from rampaging jungle beasts. The game plays like a classic shoot-em-up but is played from the perspective of a defender, not an attacker.

The target platform in mind was Android, so the controls are very simple. Just hold down mouse / touch on the screen and the player character follows your cursor and shoots.
