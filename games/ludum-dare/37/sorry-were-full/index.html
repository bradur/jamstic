Sorry, We're Full

A hunter wanders into an inn... Only to find there's only one single room in the whole inn, and it's booked!

In "Sorry, We're Full" you hunt deer during the day in order to sell their hides and meat for coins. With coins, you can purchase melee weaponry or arrows for your bow.

When you get a melee weapon, you can beat the person who booked the room before you, so that no one can stand between you and a soft, comfy bed!

Gameplay video(for those who don't know what to do):

Keys

Arrow keys / WASD to move. Left Control to shoot with the bow. Left Alt to swing the melee weapon (when you get it). ESC pauses / opens menu from which to quit / restart. Q and E do some actions. The game tells you when you need them. M to toggle music, N to toggle sfx.

Other info

  • Aiming the bow and melee weapon isn't easy. And it's quite clunky. Nevertheless, it should work as intended.
  • No WebGL build, since I ran out of time and it wouldn't build.
  • I have only tested the Windows build.
  • For best results, run it in fullscreen 16:9 (1280x720, 1920x1080, 2560x1440, etc..)
  • If your monitor isn't 16:9, run it windowed in some 16:9 aspect resolution.
  • There are bugs. Managed to weed out most of 'em, but there are still some huge ones.

Tools used

  • Unity3D (C#)
  • Sublime Text 3
  • Visual Studio Community 2015
  • Paint.NET
  • LMMS (Music)
  • bfxr (Sounds)
