A Hoppy Walk

After a rough night in your favorite watering hole it's time to go home.

Your feet are on autopilot but you have to balance yourself while staying hydrated. Make sure you don't run out of drinks and you stay on the road. Watch out for those pesky birds and banana peels!

All assets were created by us during the jam.

Features multiple endings!

Controls

Left/right arrows OR A/D - Move left/right
Q - Pause menu
